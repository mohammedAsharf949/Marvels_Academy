Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our learning plans

Can I switch between plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available?
We offer a 7-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to start your trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment.
Is there a family plan available?
Yes, we offer family plans with discounts for multiple users. Contact our sales team for more information.
